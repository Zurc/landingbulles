About Bulles d'Or . & Dorée

Bulles d’Or. focuses on developing a new baby and parents food line; freshly hand cooked food delivered to your doorstep. Through Bulles d’Or., Dorée and her team want to be like the Artisans serving the everyday and mundane needs of parents (or adults without children who like simple things). So they feel a little lighter as feathers ... or rather, as bubbles ...

A drafted portrait of Doree

"Making people’s life a little simpler and a small part of the World a little better, by getting back to Essentials"

This is what Dorée is aiming at above all!

The story tells this; she is a simple person, pleasant, funny, creative, spirited, and awfully chilled out.

What she said about herself; she is determined, generous, a tad sassy, resolutely indomitable, and greedy of life. Dorée had the idea of setting up Bulles d’Or. because she found in her daughter —Juliette Laïni, the Sweetness of her life— all the inspiration she needed to design and polish the idea. Thanks to Juliette’s needs, Bulles d’Or. was conceived as far as possible in line with the reality of a life lived when one has a child. Which entails willing to grow a child in the most amusing, interesting and healthiest fashion. Starting by the way one will feed that child, and then, add to that a great dose of love!
